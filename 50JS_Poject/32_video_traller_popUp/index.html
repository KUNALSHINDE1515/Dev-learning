Pushpa - 2 Tralier

The Rule is one of the most anticipated Indian movies, a sequel to the blockbuster Pushpa: The Rise. Starring Allu Arjun in the lead role as Pushpa Raj, the movie continues the gripping story of his rise in the red sandalwood smuggling syndicate. Fans are eagerly waiting to see what happens next in this action-packed saga, especially after the intense climax of the first part. With Rashmika Mandanna and Fahadh Faasil reprising their roles, the film promises high-octane action, powerful dialogues, and a captivating storyline. Directed by Sukumar, Pushpa 2 is set to raise the stakes and deliver a cinematic experience that will leave audiences at the edge of their seats.
